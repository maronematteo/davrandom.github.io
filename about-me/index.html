About Me


picture by Superben, thanks!

I’m interested in open source, i.e. GNU/Linux, open hardware (openmoko, arduino, …), open mapping (openstreetmap), … in music, acoustics, photography, motorbikes, traveling around, DIY, …

I have a M.Sc. in particle physics from University of Trieste (Italy).

Now I’m enrolled at UPF for a Ph.D. in Information and Communication Technologies. Meanwhile I work in a non-profit foundation called Barcelona Media.

This is not a newspaper, it’s a blog, free and open. If you want to collaborate (surrender or resist) just comment here… I will contact you directly.

I am not responsible for reader’s comments.
